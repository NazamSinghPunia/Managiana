MANGIANA

HISTORY

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Mangiana village is 060537. Mangiana village is located in Dabwali Tehsil of Sirsa district in Haryana, India. It is situated 10km away from sub-district headquarter Dabwali and 60km away from district headquarter Sirsa. As per 2009 stats, Mangeana is the gram panchayat of Mangiana village.


About MANGIANA

The total geographical area of village is 1194 hectares. Mangiana has a total population of 3,565 peoples. There are about 597 houses in Mangiana village. As per 2019 stats, Mangiana villages comes under Dabwali assembly & Sirsa parliamentary constituency. Dabwali is nearest town to Mangiana.
